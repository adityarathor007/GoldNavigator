Moving averages

The moving average (MA) is a simple technical analysis tool that smooths out price data by creating a constantly updated average price. The average is taken over a specific period of time, like 3 days, 9 days.

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis in financial news identifies the tone (positive, negative, or neutral) of news articles to gauge market sentiment, which often correlates with gold price movements. By integrating sentiment scores with quantitative data, it improves gold price prediction models by capturing the impact of economic and geopolitical factors.

LSTM

LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) is a type of recurrent neural network (RNN) designed to capture long-term dependencies in sequential data, making it ideal for time-series forecasting. We combined MAP values (3-day and 9-day) with financial news headlines for each day to make Closing gold price predictions.

R-squared

R-squared is a statistical measure of how close the data are to the fitted regression line. It is also known as the coefficient of determination, or the coefficient of multiple determination for multiple regression. The definition of R-squared is fairly straight-forward; it is the percentage of the response variable variation that is explained by a linear model. In general, the higher the R-squared, the better the model fits your data.
